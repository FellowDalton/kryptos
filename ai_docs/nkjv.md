# Christian Meditation: Entering the Secret Place

Let us consider the words of our Lord Jesus Christ in Matthew 6:6 (NKJV): "But you, when you pray, go into your room, and when you have shut your door, pray to your Father who is in the secret place; and your Father who sees in secret will reward you openly."

Our meditation practice is founded upon this divine instruction, guiding believers into the 'secret place' - a state of profound, personal communion with God. This is not a physical location, but rather a spiritual condition wherein we may experience the presence of the Almighty, hear His voice, and receive His love in a most intimate manner.

Through this practice, we seek to:

1. Cultivate Intimacy with God: By entering the 'secret place', we aim to foster a deeper, more intimate relationship with our Heavenly Father. Here, we may present ourselves openly and vulnerably, knowing that we are seen, loved, and accepted by God.

2. Focus on the Divine: In the midst of life's tumult, it is easy to lose sight of God. Our practice encourages the faithful to set aside dedicated time to focus solely on God, His Word, and His presence.

3. Experience Spiritual Growth: As we consistently enter the 'secret place', we believe that believers will experience spiritual growth. They shall gain a deeper understanding of God's character, His love for His children, and His plans for their lives.

We invite you to join us on this journey of seeking God in the 'secret place'. Let us deepen our relationship with Him, focus our hearts on His truth, and experience the spiritual growth and blessings that flow from being in His presence.
